My Love for Romance

I think I have always been a hopeless romantic. I have always enjoyed romance movies and have seen a ton. I opt for romance when choosing books. I am a sucker for a good romance in a tv show. No matter what it is, if it has any sort of romance I am in. In one of my classes I had to create a collage about the media I consume and more than half of the things included involve romance.
